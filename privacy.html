Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Overview

Krishna Journal is built with your privacy as a top priority. We collect no personal identifying information whatsoever. Your journal entries, emotions, and reflections are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

2. What We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location
  • Your journal entries or emotional data (these stay on your device only)
  • Any account credentials (no accounts required)

Anonymous data that MAY be collected by third-party SDKs (see Section 3):

  • App crash reports
  • Anonymous purchase and subscription status (no personal identifiers)

3. Third-Party Services

This app integrates the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies for full details:

Anthropic (Claude API)

When you request Krishna's guidance, your selected emotion and optional context text are sent over HTTPS to Anthropic's API to generate a response. We do not store this data on our servers. Anthropic may retain API inputs per their own data policy.

See: anthropic.com/privacy

RevenueCat

Manages in-app subscriptions and purchase status. Collects anonymous device identifiers and purchase history. No personally identifiable information is shared with RevenueCat.

See: revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple (App Store / StoreKit)

Standard App Store payment processing. Governed entirely by Apple's privacy policy.

See: apple.com/privacy

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All journal entries and settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted on-device storage
  • No cloud sync or backup is performed by Krishna Journal
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • You can permanently delete all your data at any time: Open the app → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All Data

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, no data is stored on our end.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
