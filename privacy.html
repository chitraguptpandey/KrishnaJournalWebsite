Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters deeply to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Krishna Journal ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name you choose.
  • Journal Entries: The text you write in your journal is stored encrypted on our servers so you can access it across devices.
  • Emotion & Mood Data: Emotion tags and mood ratings you submit to receive shloka recommendations.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous, aggregated data about how features are used (e.g., which sections are visited most). We use third-party SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics) for this purpose. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Device Information: Operating system version, app version, and crash logs to help us fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We never receive or store full payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Krishna Journal app and its features.
  • Match your emotional context to relevant Bhagavad Gita shlokas via our AI service.
  • Sync your journal entries across your devices.
  • Send you optional push notifications (daily reminders) that you configure in the app.
  • Respond to your support requests and communicate important service updates.
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve the user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Encryption at rest: Your journal entries are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored on our servers.
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access controls: Only authorized personnel have access to production systems, and all access is logged and audited.
  • Regular security reviews: We periodically review our security practices and update them as needed.

While we take every precaution, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your device secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Krishna Journal uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Powers our AI shloka-matching feature. Emotional context you share is sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalized guidance. Anthropic does not use this data to train their models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication, anonymous analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Handles subscription billing and in-app purchases.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription state and entitlements across platforms.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and all associated data. You can initiate this from the app's Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Portability: Request an export of your journal entries in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of analytics: Disable anonymous analytics collection from Settings → Privacy.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California (CCPA), additional rights may apply. Please contact us for assistance.

6. Children's Privacy

Krishna Journal is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending you an email. Your continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out:

  • Support page: support.html
  • Email: privacy@krishnajournal.app
